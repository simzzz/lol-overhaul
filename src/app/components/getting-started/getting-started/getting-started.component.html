Getting Started

Welcome to League of Legends, summoner. Here you'll find all the basic information you need to get set up and hit the Fields of Justice.

New Player Guide

The new player guide will introduce you to the control scheme and core League of Legends gameplay. It will also walk you through the in-game tutorials and your first battles on the Fields of Justice. From competition against the bots to your first head to head experience, the new player guide will help you learn the skills you need to take on other summoners.

Become a Summoner

You can customize your summoner through different summoner spells or runes resulting in thousands of different combinations for your champ so you can find the one that suits your individual playstyle.

Chat Commands

Find out all the basic commands you can use in chat in order to optimize your communication as much as possible.

Interracting with Players

Through PvP.net you'll have access to a number of social features that help you find teammates, communicate with other summoners and invite your real life friends to play.

Summoners Code

Here you will learn the summoner's code. It will teach you how to become a teamplayer and be friendly to the community. All players are oblidged to follow the summoner's code and breaking it will result in severe penalties.
